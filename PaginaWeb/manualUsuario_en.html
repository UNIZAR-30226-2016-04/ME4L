Purpose food recipe

Fill alL the fields of your recipe and click on "Enviar Receta Propuesta" so we can validate it. All the ingredients that you can imagine are on our databases (If u miss any, contact us).

Admin

Modify recipe

As admin you will be able to modify a recipe already added. Once chose the recipe to modify a new purpose of recipe is made.

Advanced search

A big amount of recipes are stored in our Database just from one click. Choose the number of persons yoi want to make the recipe and the main ingredient to start your search.

Add recipe

As administrator you could add recipes in a direct way without going through validation step. The recipe is add automatically to our database.

Download and use

It is not needed any installation, so once you get the aplication downloaded you can already start using it. Download it is very simple, you just need to download aplication's jar, don't need any adiitional libraries or documents. It weigths it is really low so download is quite fast!
