¿Quiénes somos?

Friends for Software is a company formed by five Informatic Enginners of the University of Zaragoza. This company was born in 2016 to develop the APP ME4L (My Election For Lunch).

We are Jorge Sanz, Alberto Martinez, Álvaro Monteagudo, Pablo Viñuales, Carlos Tolón y Javier Sabaté (Scholar) a group of young engineers who has taken their knowledge at EINA and their academic experience has Android. Web, and JAVA applications development courses. ME4L, it is porgrammed in JAVA.

My Election For Lunch, ME4L

The aplication may be one of the most used applications that users would use to chose what food to do, this is due to its simple use and intuitive interface that makes for the users a great and interactive experience without being techonology experts.

Our team is always willing to help in ME4L use or download, so with our user manual u could check how easy is to share ur food recipes. Moreover if u have any doubt u can write us, we will be please to solve your issues. You can also send some suggestions to improve ME4L, we will take them in count for the future of our company.
